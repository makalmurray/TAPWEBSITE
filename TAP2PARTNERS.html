NSCC stands for Nova Scotia Community Collage. With multiple differant locations offering 140+ programs, NSCC as a lot of variety. NSCC and one of the big partners of TAP. NSCC helps organize TAP events and projects to keep students involded. They held a class for 3 weeks for TAP students to learn how to code in July 2021 Students learned HTML, CSS, and a bit of JAVASCRIPT. With the TAP program, NSCC offers a 2 year diploma in the IT field that students are rewarded at the end of high school.
